2025 US Crypto Tax Guidelines

Short-term Capital Gains

Held < 1 year. Taxed as ordinary income at your federal tax bracket (10%-37%).

Long-term Capital Gains

Held > 1 year. Taxed at 0%, 15%, or 20% based on total taxable income.

Remember: Wash sale rules don't currently apply to crypto, but proposed regulations may change this. Always verify current IRS rules.
